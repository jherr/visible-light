Queer Eye for the Straight Guy

16 July 2003

What a hoot this new show is on Bravo. The 5 gay men take over a straight guy's life and show him style. The episode I watch took an artist in NY who lived in overalls and helped him find a comfortable style. They also cleaned his apartment, took him to get a haircut, style, gave him tips on conversing in a party, and finally the chef of the group helped him cook mini pizza's. The best part was the dish session at the end where they watched him goto his first gallery showing for his work and commented on how looked, behaved etc. I'm not giving it justice and it'll get old fast but it was a funny show.
