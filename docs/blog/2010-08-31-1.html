adjustments

31 August 2010

Mumsie, my dad's lovely wife, makes a really great poppy seed cake that people rave about. When I had it I asked for the recipe and I've been passing it off as my own here in California, and I get all the praise.

So cut to tonight .. I planned on making it for the Principal Coffee for tomorrow. I bought the main ingredient, and after dinner tonight I went and printed up the copy of the recipe from my email. Well poppy seed cake - you need poppy seeds, but I didn't have any so now I'm making pound cake instead (same recipe no Poppy Seeds). I start mixing together slowing going down the list … eggs oil… etc.. when I get to sour cream.. Again - no sour cream… so what to do … I substituted Buttermilk instead. It smells great and it baked beautifully.

I'm just wondering when I get to really call the recipe as my own.
