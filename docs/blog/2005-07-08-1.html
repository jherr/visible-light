Strange days

08 July 2005

Yesterday was a strange day indeed. I woke up to a broken toilet in our (the tank won't fill with water). The plumber is coming today but I had to play phone tag with them for most of the day to get that set. Sadie's $150 meds I'd opened wed evening smelled like cat pee and when i opened the reserve bottle from the same lot it too smelled so I had to run to the vet and return them, only they had no other so she's med free until they get a new batch in. Both of these odd, but even odder happening together.

But the strangest thing was After I put Megan down last nigh, I was installing Megan's carseat back in my car (we had my car serviced). I was accosted by two of the cutest little white dogs. I couldn't lock them in our side yard cause they escaped so I put them in my car and waited for the dog catcher. I do hope their owner finds them, or they go to a great home. they were so loveable.
