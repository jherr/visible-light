mimi experiment - failure

18 December 2010

I haven't had time to run Mimi in 3 days. yesterday I came home from picking up Mimi to find her on the sofa back paw in the air and urine all around her. She was in obvious pain and couldn't put any weight on her leg. I rushed her to the vet. She has patellar luxation , which had completely popped out. The vet popped it back in, took XRays and gave her a shot for the pain. She is to be kept from being too active and we have medication. I'm also supposed to Ice it and then heat it. Icing means I hold her against me with an ice pack between me and her. Heating won't be hard - the dog finds the heating pad every time its turned on.

Mimi Experiment - success!

15 December 2010

Its the third day of me biking and Mimi running and I declare it a success. Today we went a mile, with her running full tilt all the way. Monday we did a mile and half. Though she was definitely dragging by the end then. So her limit seems to be about a mile. But that is pretty good for a dog that weighs in under 20 lbs. And she sleeps great - though she still found energy to destroy my glasses.
