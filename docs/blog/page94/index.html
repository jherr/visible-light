Sadie update for all those who are wondering...

27 March 2006

Sadie is doing great - better than great. Her neck is completely better. I had her groomed on Friday last week and now with her short hair cut she's prancing and acting like a 2 year old. Its great to see. She's also seems to be settling in her age (finally!) Tonight she's sleeping on the sofa while I'm watching TV. I always knew she'd sleep on the sofa, but she usually gets down when someone comes in the room and she has never gotten up there while we've had the TV on. Its wonderful to see that she finally feels at home here. After years of abuse before we got her, she really didn't trust that we wouldn't hurt her when we'd find her up on furniture. She was the first dog i'd ever seen have a panic attack. But now she's sleeping on the sofa - with one leg up in the air, and I know that she is finally at home.

Bird day afternoon

23 March 2006

I surprised Megan this afternoon when I picked her up from school today. I had packed us a picnic and we headed over to the local historical farm. After eating lunch in the company of a peacock, who was very interested in us, we wondered around and looked at all the animals. Megan befriend a horse who was breaking his neck to get to the tall grass on the other side of the fence. She happily pulled it out and fed him, same with the sheep, though most stayed clear of us - only one actually wanted the grass she presented. While we were walking back out a couple of hummingbirds flew by us. It stopped me in my tracks and Megan ask me to point out the birds to her, and when I tried she just couldn't see them, so small. So after we left we headed over to a local bird store (for bird watchers not owners) and we bought a humming bird feeder. Once home we set it up, and I also filled all the other feeders.
