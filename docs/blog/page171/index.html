In Atlanta

16 July 2004

We arrived in Atlanta, 15 mins early. I managed to get Megan, her car seat, our 50lb carry bag and the stroller all the way to the Rental car counter. We upgraded our car to a SUV, found a sky cab and he helped me get our 4 checked bags to the shuttle bus to the Hertz land. The driver of the bus dropped us off at the car, loaded all our bags and even helped me get the darn car seat installed. We then headed the mile back to the airport and went about one mile beyond to the hotel. Embassy suites is by reputation a fairly nice chain. This one is a tad ragged around the edges, and not as clean as I would normally like, but I'm tired and I don't really care.
