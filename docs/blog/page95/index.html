Sadie girl

10 March 2006

Sadie has a big swollen face. I took her to the vet this morning after watching it grow for 2 days and not really comprehending that there was a problem. I just kept saying to myself - man Sadie's putting on weight, or in this light Sadie looks strange. Then last night I finally felt for myself under her chin and realized she had a growth of some sort. The vet thinks its an infection but did see a few questionable cells under the microscope. So we've sent it off to a lab to be looked at, and meanwhile she's on antibiotics. I tell you she's a brave dog but I'm not sure how much more she can take. - as long as she's comfortable and happy.

guilty pleasure

09 March 2006

one of my guilty pleasures is Project Runway and I'm so happy that Chloe won it. Her collection was beautiful - so refined and cohesive.
