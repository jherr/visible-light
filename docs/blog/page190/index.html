CARNIVALE

22 October 2003

HBO has done it again. First Sopranos, then Six Feet Under, now another show that is really turning out to be one of the best on TV with superb writing and acting. Carnivale , takes place during the depression in the dust bowl. Its follows a Carni troop and tells their very mixed up stories. This weeks episode ended a 2 parter -SPOILER ALERT- that had the Carnivale coming to a old mining town. There they performed for an all male audience. The girly show was a big hit and when one of the performers stripped down all the way, the miners went nuts. Next thing they know she's been killed and the Carni folk are seeking retribution only no one is around to give it. As they try to figure out what is going on, you realize that all the miners are dead, and they have killed the "harlot" to have a woman to entertain them in entirety. I shudder for the girl - and this is fiction. As they left the town they showed her image in a window being pulled back in the room by a dirty miners arm.

DOLLAR STORE FIND

20 October 2003

On Sunday while searching through our cd collection I rediscovered a dollar store find. Back when Jack and I were first married, I frequented the dollar store down in Cutler Ridge Mall, maybe we were dating cause Hurricane Andrew blew the Cutler Ridge Mall away and the reopened much smaller mall later didn't have the dollar store. Anyway one of the many things I would do was buy cd's from the dollar store and then try and resell them to CD Warehouse for 5 bucks. I know not very good and I think all told they only bought 1 cd from all my attempts. One of the ones I was stuck with was David Broza "Away From Home" Well I tried it and loved it. Its a collection of American poetry by contemporary poets put to soulful folk music. Its really well done and I"m glad I've re-found it. The link here isn't for the same CD as it doesn't appear to be for sale anymore.
