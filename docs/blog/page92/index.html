The pit of despair...

14 April 2006

Megan and I joined playgroup today for an open play time at a local gymnastics place. We had a blast and will definitely do it again. One of the highlights was this big pit of foam cubes each about a foot long. The pit is probably 6 or 7 feet deep with no stairs or steps like a pool. I sat with my feet dangling in when my sock got eaten by some of the foam. So not thinking I went in after it. And well … I got stuck. It took me what seemed like an hour but was probably just a couple of minutes to build a step high enough to give me a good leg up. Each time I tried I would smooch the the cubes and sink further in..I never felt the bottom. I did get out, but not before another mom got a picture… I'll post it if she emails it to me :)

Friends like me

14 April 2006

This past tuesday night I went out with a couple of mom friends of mine. We did dinner and then headed over to the local mega-plex to see a film. We'd planned on seeing Lucky number sleven instead. Now my friend Kari doesn't care for dramas or violence so I was surprised she agreed to see this particular film. She's the reason I usually see every comedy that comes out. Anyway she was all for it until we approached to buy our tickets. I was first in line so while I was getting my ticket she asked the gril from behind me if "Sleven" was a comedy or not. I looked at the girl with big eyes and mouthed "Please tell her YES" well the girl did one better and was so convincing that Kari's fears were laid to rest until the opening credits when 3 people are murdered and very violently too.

a great concert

11 April 2006

I went to see Anna Nalick in concert on Sunday night with Jeanetta our Baby sitter. The concert was really wonderful. One of the better concerts in my life time. The venue was smallish and intimate. It reminded me of my college groupie days and made me feel a tad old.
