Sunday lovely sunday

11 June 2006

I had my film club today - let me first tell you that I got an email from the clubs director informing me that San Jose was having a gay pride parade and parking would be scarce - so arrive early. I drove down at 11:30 - show time is 2. I got down by noon and well the parade was long over and everything was cleaned up. Downtown seemed almost diserted. Since i'd had a big breakfast with Jack and Megan this morning, I wasn't really in the mood for lunch. I decided to just walk around Down Town San Jose. First I found a drug store and managed to waste alittle time there. Then walking further I found the San Jose Museum of Art . Well still having over an hour and half to kill I decided to wonder on in. I really enjoyed myself. Though I can honestly say i'm not a hugh fan of "modern" art, I did enjoy seeing all the different pieces. And I took my time, figuring I could come back next film club if I didn't see it all, but as it turned out the museum is small and I did.
