It took her 4 nights to go from sleeping in one very small quarter of the full size bed to spreading out and pretty much sleeping all over the upper half. She seems to love the duvet, though when she was in the crib I couldn't seem to keep a blanket on her. She was sick on Sunday after we had a lovely day at Del Valle park in Livermore. We went swimming and Jack and Megan went boating, and we had a lovely picnic and the all the kids from 5 different families played. It was such a "Norman Rockwell" type of sunday. But when we got home Megan was drained and obviously not feeling well. I figured she was dehydrated but by 2am she had 102 fever.
