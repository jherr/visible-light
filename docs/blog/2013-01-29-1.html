just like that...

29 January 2013

Last year we started walking the dogs every evening after dinner. Now they expect it. But I've been feeling better and Then as Oso started declining, I took to giving him regular walks. Slow and deliberate - they really cheered him up. After he died, I took to taking out the little dogs one at a time in the morning. Last week, I managed a mile with each dog in the morning and just like that I was doing 2 miles plus of walks a day.

Then yesterday I did my 2 miles, and then Megan wanted to goto park and use her new long board and just like that I'd done a 5K.

So today I just did it - 2 miles with the dogs and an extra in the afternoon before pickup. Still short of the 5k by a bit, but If I had time, I could do that.

Getting the dogs used to walking in the morning means I can't back out. But when it warms up and I start biking 4 miles round trip to Megan's school, 2 mile walks might be too much.

And just like that I'm doing 2 miles to 5K a day.
